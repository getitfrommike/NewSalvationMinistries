We at New Salvation Ministries, Inc. are called to rise to the cry of the poor and homeless.

Our #1 goal is to honor and reverence God for who He is — the creator and sustainer of all things.

God’s will MUST be done on Earth as it is in Heaven.

Our desire is to be a refuge of the homeless population in the metropolitan L.A. area.

Soon, we will be offering showers for the homeless.

Our MAIN service is:
URBAN STREET CHURCH ON SKID ROW, CALIFORNIA — every second Saturday on the corner of 5th and Crocker Street. We provide the Gospel of Jesus, Bibles, clothing, shoes, snacks, a hot lunch, a lot of love, and haircuts… all thanks to God our Father. Amen.

We also serve the All Tribes Sonlight Mission in Poston, Arizona. Our annual outreach takes a truckload of supplies to the Colorado River Indian Reservation — clothing, food supplies, water — every 3rd Saturday in December, right before Christmas.

We are also a GRASS ROOTS, END TIME — FRONT LINE MINISTRY.
