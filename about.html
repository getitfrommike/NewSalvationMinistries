New Salvation Ministries started about December 2000 at the North County Correctional Facility (Super Max). I was called by God in accordance to 2 Timothy 4:1-5.

God called me to preach and teach my fellow inmates. Isaiah 43:18-19 says, “Remember not the former things of old, for behold, I will do a new thing and now it shall spring forth. Shall you not know it?”

New Salvation Ministries is the new salvation Jesus provided to all who will call upon His name. Ministry is what God called us to do as professing Christians according to Matthew 28:19 — to fulfill the Great Commission.
